# DeutschHub Product

## 1. Vision

- DeutschHub is a platform that accompanies Vietnamese learners throughout their journey to Germany.

- Instead of focusing only on language learning, DeutschHub combines German language education, cultural exploration, study abroad guidance and practical knowledge into one connected learning experience.

- Our goal is to help learners spend less time searching for information and more time understanding Germany.

## 2. Problem Statement

- People who are interested in Germany often need to search across multiple platforms for information.

- Language learning is separated from culture.

- Study abroad information is scattered.

- Practical knowledge is difficult to verify.

- This fragmented experience makes learning inefficient and time-consuming.

## 3. Solution

- DeutschHub centralizes trustworthy resources into one platform.

Users can:

- Learn German.

- Explore German culture.

- Read study abroad guides.

- Discover cities and daily life.

- Track learning progress.

Everything is connected to support one continuous learning journey.

## 4. Target Users

- People who want to learn German.

- Students preparing to study in Germany.

- People planning to work in Germany.

- Travelers interested in German culture.

- Anyone curious about Germany.

## 5. Core Values

- Reliable Information

- Connected Learning

- User-first Experience

- Long-term Learning

- Simplicity

## 6. Product Scope (Version 1)

- Language Courses

- Learning Progress

- Quiz

- Germany Knowledge Base

- Study Abroad Resources

- Course Certificates

- Authentication

- Personal Profile

## 7. Product Principles

- Every feature must help users move one step closer to Germany.

- Learning should always be connected with real-life context.

- Trustworthy information is more important than information quantity.

- Keep the experience simple, focused and enjoyable.

## Product Philosophy

- DeutschHub is not only a platform to learn German, but a platform to understand Germany.
